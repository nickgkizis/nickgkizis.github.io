My Story

I am a programmer from Greece,
but that is only a part of my journey.

Growing up I had a diverse set of interests,
which probably had an impact on my story.

I studied Philology and Linguistics at
Aristotle University, in my hometown, Thessaloniki.
After I graduated I felt something was missing,
I wanted to transition towards something more technical.

My curiosity led me to move to Sweden
where I studied at Uppsala University,
and earn my Master's degree in Digital Humanities,
a field that explores the digitalization of cultural heritage.

Moving abroad was a challenge on its own,
let alone studying and working in a foreign country.
But no matter the challenge,
I felt that I was making my dreams come to life.

After my graduation I felt confident that I knew exactly
what I wanted to do, that being becoming a web developer!
To achieve my goal I started a course about Full-Stack Development
at Athens University of Economics and Business.
